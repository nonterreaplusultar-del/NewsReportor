中文：
赋予美国情报机构无证监控外国人通信权力的《外国情报监视法》702条款于本周五到期。隐私倡导者视为重大胜利，但实际影响有限：现有监控项目的认证将持续到2027年3月。电子前哨基金会（EFF）警告，尽管该条款形式上失效，庞大的监控机器并不会因此停摆。

English:
Section 702 of FISA, which allows warrantless surveillance of non-Americans, has officially expired. While celebrated by digital rights groups like the EFF, the practical impact is minimal, as existing surveillance certifications remain valid until March 2027. The underlying spying apparatus, analysts note, is unchanged.
